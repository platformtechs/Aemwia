AEMWIA is an Organization of SOUTH AFRICA brings together young men and women from all walks of life in pursuit of its noble objectives in the service of children across the globe for a better world socio-economic and political order.It is a great startup which provides shelter, Education and growth to Orphans and sick for their betterment and comfortable life.

AEMWIA national level development organization with its welfare projects spread across SOUTH AFRICA.

AEMWIA is an NGO in SOUTH AFRICA directly benefitting over 600,000 children every year through more than 250 live welfare projects on education, healthcare, livelihood and women empowerment, in over 950 remote villages and slumsacross 25 states of SOUTH AFRICA.
